Chickpea Salad

About the Appetizer

This easy salad recipe is the perfect side dish for almost any meal, or a great lunch. It's packed with protein and nutrients, and the dressing is fresh and needs just a few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups grape tomatoes (halved)
  • 2 cups diced cucumber
  • 15.5 ounces chickpeas (drained and rinsed, 1 can)
  • 3/4 cup diced green bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup finely diced red onion
  • 1/2 lemon (juiced)

Dressing

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 avocado (optional)
